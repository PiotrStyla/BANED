Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better user experience and understand how our service is used.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
cookie_consent Stores your cookie consent preferences 1 year
session_id Maintains your session state Session

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website. All data is anonymized.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our service is hosted on Vercel, which may set its own cookies for performance and security purposes:

  • Vercel Analytics: Performance monitoring
  • CDN Cookies: Content delivery optimization

4. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage for:

  • Saving user preferences (theme, language)
  • Caching recent analysis results
  • Reducing server load

Note: Local storage data never leaves your device.

5. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our service
  • Improve service performance
  • Prevent abuse and ensure security
  • Provide personalized experience

6. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

Our Cookie Banner

When you first visit our site, you can choose which cookies to accept. You can change your preferences anytime by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer.

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies (may affect functionality)
  • Clear cookies when closing browser

Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

7. Do Not Track (DNT)

We respect "Do Not Track" signals. If your browser has DNT enabled, we will not use analytics cookies.

8. Cookie Consent

By using our website, you consent to our use of essential cookies. For optional cookies, we request your explicit consent through our cookie banner.

You can withdraw consent at any time by:

  • Clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer
  • Clearing your browser cookies
  • Disabling cookies in browser settings

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
