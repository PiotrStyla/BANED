Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to BANED (Bayesian-Augmented News Evaluation and Detection). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our fake news detection service.

2. Information We Collect

Text Analysis Data:

  • Text content you submit for analysis (temporarily processed, not stored)
  • Analysis results and predictions

Technical Data:

  • IP address (for rate limiting and security)
  • Browser type and version
  • Time zone and location data
  • Access times and referring URLs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide fake news detection services
  • Improve our AI models and detection accuracy
  • Prevent abuse and ensure service security
  • Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Retention

Submitted Text: Text submitted for analysis is processed in real-time and is NOT permanently stored on our servers. It exists only temporarily in memory during processing.

Analysis Logs: We may retain anonymized analysis metadata (without the actual text content) for service improvement and security purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: With hosting providers (Vercel) who help operate our service
  • Anonymous Research: Anonymized, aggregated data for academic research

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Cookie consent preferences
  • Session management
  • Basic analytics (anonymized)

See our Cookie Policy for detailed information.

7. Third-Party Services

Our service is hosted on Vercel. Please review their privacy policy:

  • Vercel Privacy Policy

8. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure serverless architecture
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in servers located in different countries where Vercel operates. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
